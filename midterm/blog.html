Tutorial

Never used Gel Polish before? Follow our simple instructions to achieve the perfect manicure.

Step 1 Prep

Prep the natural nail by first removing any nail enhancements including: gel polish,acrylic extensions,polygel etc. Removal methods can e-file nail removal and soak off technique with acetone.

One you see the natural nail remove the shine of the nail plate by buffing the nail in short strokes with a 180 grit buffing block. This will allow the gel to stick better and longer to the natural nail.

Placeholder

Step 2 Prime

Soak a lint free cotton pad such has kitchen towels in alcohol and wipe the nail plate to remove any residue or oil. Allow to dry completely

Next, apply a coat of PH bonder to each nail and allow to dry for a minimum of 60 sec.Then, apply a coat of base gel and cure for 30 sec. in a LED lamp and 1 min. in a UV lamp.

Placeholder

Step 3 Apply

Apply a thin layer of our gel color to the nail. Cure in your nail lamp immediately one nail at a time. Repeat until desired coverage is achieved.

Warning: painting all nails then curing can cause uneven application.

Step 4 Seal

Apply our no wipe topcoat and then then cure.
